Comprehensive Management Features

Designed to streamline institution management with efficiency and ease

Task Management

Effortlessly assign and track tasks for students and staff with our intuitive system. Set deadlines, priorities, and receive notifications.

Library Access

Manage library resources, track borrowing, and maintain records with ease. Digital catalog and automated reminders for returns.

Transport Tracking

Real-time monitoring of institution transport schedules and vehicle locations. Parents can track buses and receive arrival alerts.

Lifetime Student Profile

Maintain comprehensive student records that follow them throughout their educational journey, from enrollment to alumni status.

Live Transportation System

Real-time GPS tracking of school buses with live updates for parents and administrators. Receive alerts for delays and route changes.

Hostel Management

Complete hostel management system with room allocation, meal planning, attendance tracking, and maintenance requests.
